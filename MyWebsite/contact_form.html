About Me

My name is Colin Beltz and I currently work in Customer Service for the State of Minnesota.

My goal is to work in web development because I would like to get back to creating projects with a group as well as on my own.

I used to work in the newspaper industry. At different points during my career, I conducted interviews, wrote stories, shot and edited photographs, edited copy and designed news pages in advance as well as on deadline.

Three important things to know about me:

  • I enjoy collaborating with others.
  • Working hard can be fun.
  • I may be quiet, but that does not mean I don't have good ideas.

One of my favorite websites to visit is Twins Daily. I enjoy following the Twins and it is a great source for news and stories about the team.
