How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Get peanut butter, jelly and two pieces of bread.
  2. Take one piece of bread and spread peanut butter on it.
  3. Then spread jelly on to of the peanut butter.
  4. Put the other peice of bread on top.
  5. Eat!
