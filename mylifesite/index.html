My Life

When I wake up in the morning

Each morning I wake up, I am disappointed.

I went to bed I hoping I would never wake up.

So now I have to get through today.

During the day

On workdays, I cannot wait until the day is over. My job is to help people.

But I feel like I should be working on my own issues. But I don't know how.

All I hear is just go get a new job. Apparently this is easy.

At night

My dream is to be a developer. It does not have a date, so it cannot be a goal yet.

I've built a few websites here and there over the past many years I have had this dream.

I haven't showed them to many people. They get rejected and so I get dejected and stop coding.

Eventually I start again, but have lost the momentum I once had.

Hopefully, tomorrow will be different. Hopefully, there won't be one.
