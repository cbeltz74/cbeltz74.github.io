Welcome!

One of my favorite projects has been getting a Topps flagship card for each year the Twins have been in existance.

Since the Twins have been in Minnesota for more than 60 years, I thought it would be fun to make this a list of the best Twins ever.

As with many of my early projects, cost was something I took into consideration. I focused on cards toward the end of a player's Twins career. Not only would the cards likely to be less expensive, but the card backs would include a large amount of thier stats as Twins.

This didn't always work out this way. But it was fun to look at several lists and books to see who the best Twins of the last 60 years have been.

Use the links above to see the cards, which are arranged by decade.

I plan to come back to this project and move certain players around as well as add new ones.

Thanks for looking!
